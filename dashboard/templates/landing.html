Autonomous Driving

Manage your Car

To access your vehicle's dashboard:

  1. Power on the Raspberry Pi and Servo in your vehicle's AI/Sensors package
  2. After about 1 minute and 30 seconds, refresh this page. You should now see your vehicle's name in the drop-down menu below
  3. Select your vehicle's name to be taken to your dashboard
  4. You will see your car's id, video feed, and the following options:
    • 'Start' - signals vehicle to start autonomously driving
    • 'Stop' - signals vehicle to stop driving
    • 'Lane Color Selection' - set the color of the lanes. Make to check for areas of different lighting
    • 'Disable/Enable Streaming' - toggle video stream. Disabling streaming can improve performance
    • 'Driving Direction' - toggle driving direction to accommodate motor placement
    • 'Top Speed' - change your vehicle's top speed from 0-100 percent of full throttle
      *** driving at high speeds can result in mechanical damage ***
    • 'Steering Aggressiveness' - change steering aggressiveness from 50-200 percent of normal steering
      *** over-steering can result in mechanical damage ***
